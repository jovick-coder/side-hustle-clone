What you stand to benefit

Some of the benefits of being a participant of the Side Hustle internship include:

Earn 3 certificates

One from Side Hustle, One from Jobberman, One from Udemy.

Learn from the very best for free

All mentors of the Side Hustle internship have received one form of training or the other and are all professionals in their fields.

Get a Jobberman soft skills scholarship

You’ll also have free access to the Jobberman soft skills scholarship, which gives you high priority when you apply for jobs on the platform.

Network with like minds

During this internship, you will be tasked with joining teams, which gives you unlimited access to forming strong networks with like minds.

Be a part of a fast-growing startup

As an intern, you stand a chance to become a part of the Side Hustle team or any other fast paced start up after your training.

Earn perfomance-based stipends

During this internship, high performing interns from all tracks will earn stipends and perks based on their performance in tasks given.
